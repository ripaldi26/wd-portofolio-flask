A Bedtime Stories application is an app that provides a collection of bedtime stories for children. These apps typically offer short stories, or calming narratives designed to help users relax and fall asleep more easily. Mini Games

Guessing image mini games are simple games where players must guess or identify an image based on provided clues. Typically, the images are presented in the form of fragments, or edited pictures. Players can earn points or rewards based on the speed and accuracy of their guesses. These games are often designed to be fun and challenging, suitable for all ages.
